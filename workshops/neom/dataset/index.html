1 Context

Evaluating university faculty teaching and research performance is necessary for multiple reasons, including assurance of student learning and informing administrative decision-making.

In this workshop, we will use a simulated dataset consisting of faculty employees information. The data set will be use for learning data visualization in R. The dataset is available in ´xlsx´ and you can download it here.

2 Content

The dataset contains 32 variables and 850 rows of data. It reports data metrics related to four different dimensions: human resources, teaching performance, research performance and services.

Element Description
Human Resources
Personal information Personal information of the faculty members
Employmnet information Information related to appointments of faculty members
Teaching
Effectiveness of course delivery The extent to which knowledge presented is valid. Course content includes: course readings, instructor-developed resources, class exercises, clinical practice, and experiments
Effectiveness in Managing Multiple Courses Ability to manage multuple courses
Effectiveness in Mentoring Students Ability to encourage students and to help them understand the factors that contribute to academic success
Research
Productivity in Research Capacity to conduct research that is of a high enough quality to regularly appear in refereed publications and be regularly included in conference presentations
Service
Service to the Institution Involvement in university committees
Service to Society Engaging in community outreach or community engagement effort
